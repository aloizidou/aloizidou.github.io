About RootSquare

Our Company Snapshot

RootSquare is an AI company building systems that reason under uncertainty. We combine Large Language Models with Bayesian Networks to replace black-box predictions with structured, causal intelligence.Most AI systems fail when data is incomplete, biased, or noisy. That is exactly where real-world decisions happen.

Our hybrid architecture is designed for those conditions. It handles missing information, models cause-and-effect relationships, and generates explainable outputs instead of pattern-based guesses.Our work is grounded in peer-reviewed research and academic collaboration. We build resource-efficient systems that are transparent, scalable, and deployable in practical environments.

RootSquare exists to make AI reliable, interpretable, and operational in the real world.
