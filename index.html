FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

We are often asked...

What does RootSquare do?

RootSquare builds AI systems that clean, connect, and explain complex data. Our work focuses on data deduplication, multimodal intelligence, and explainable healthcare AI.

What problem are you solving?

Real-world data is messy, fragmented, and often wrong. Duplicate records, missing information, and disconnected sources lead to bad decisions. We build AI that restores clarity and trust in data.

Is RootSquare a healthcare company or an AI company?

Both. We are an AI research and engineering company with a strong focus on healthcare and humanitarian applications where data integrity and explainability are critical.

What is data deduplication and why does it matter?

Data deduplication is the process of identifying and resolving multiple records that refer to the same individual. In healthcare and nonprofits, duplicates can lead to safety risks, wasted resources, and incorrect decisions.

How is your approach different from traditional AI models?

Our systems combine machine learning, multimodal reasoning, and causal models. This allows us to handle missing data, reduce bias, and provide explanations instead of black-box predictions.
