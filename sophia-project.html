What This Project Is:

‍

This project is a decision-support chatbot built on Bayesian Networks. Instead of generating answers based on patterns alone, the chatbot reasons under uncertainty. It models relationships between variables and updates its beliefs as new information is provided by the user.

The chatbot does not “guess.” It infers probabilities.

Each user input updates the network, refining the system’s understanding of the situation and leading to more informed, explainable responses.

‍

What It Helps With

‍

This approach is especially useful in scenarios where:

  • Information is incomplete or noisy
  • Multiple factors influence an outcome
  • Decisions must be justified, not just predicted

The chatbot helps users explore complex decision spaces step by step. It narrows down possibilities, highlights the most influential factors, and adapts dynamically as new answers are given.

Instead of overwhelming users with all possibilities, it guides them toward the most likely outcomes based on evidence.

‍

Why Using Bayesian Networks Is the Key Advantage

‍

Most chatbots rely on black-box language models. Our chatbot is built on a causal probabilistic structure.

This gives three major advantages:

  1. Interpretability
    Every conclusion comes from explicit relationships and probability updates. The reasoning can be inspected, explained, and trusted.
  2. Robustness to Uncertainty
    Bayesian Networks are designed for missing or partial information. The chatbot still works even when the user does not know all the answers.
  3. Human-Aligned Reasoning
    The system reasons in a way that mirrors how humans think under uncertainty, updating beliefs rather than producing fixed outputs.

The Big Win

‍

This chatbot is not just conversational. It is analytical.

It combines natural interaction with principled probabilistic reasoning, making it ideal for domains where correctness, transparency, and decision support matter more than creative text generation.

In short, it is a chatbot that reasons, not one that merely responds.
